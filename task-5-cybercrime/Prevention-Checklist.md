# Stay Safe Online - Checklist

This checklist provides important safety tips for college students to prevent cybercrime and stay secure online.

## General Safety Tips
- Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication
- Do not share personal information such as address, phone number, or passwords online
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading files from unknown sources
- Verify information before sharing to prevent spreading fake news
- Keep your devices and apps updated with the latest security patches.
- Log out from shared or public devices after use.

## Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone under any circumstances
- Remember that receiving money does NOT require entering your UPI PIN
- Do not scan unknown QR codes or approve payment requests from unknown sources
- Always verify the identity of the person before making any digital payment

## Reporting CyberCrime
- Report cyber fraud at: https://cybercrime.gov.in/
- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930

## Reminder 
Stay alert, think before you click, and protect your digital identity
