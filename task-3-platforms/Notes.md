## Internet Safety Awareness
I have come to understand how important it is to practice safe internet habits.
I have learned the following:
- Passwords are important.
- Two-factor authentication provides an extra layer of security.
- After using a computer, it is important to log out so that someone else doesn’t wobble into your account.
I have also come to understand some of the common things that people do:
- They click on strange links.
- They share passwords.
- They share information with everyone.
This shows how easy it is to get into trouble and compromise security. Safe internet habits have become really important to me.
